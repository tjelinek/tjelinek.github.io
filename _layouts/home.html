I am a Ph.D. student at the Czech Technical University in Prague working in the visual recognition group (VRG) of Prof. Jiri Matas. My research focus is 3D reconstruction and tracking of unknown objects in videos. I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Computer Science from Masaryk University, where I worked as a student research assistant under the supervision of Vlad Popovici.
